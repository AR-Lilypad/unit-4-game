Crystals Collector!

The Game:


The game opens with a random number displayed on the screen.

Your objective is to match your score to the random number.

There are four crystals to choose from. Click on a crystal to add a specific number of points to your score to your board.

You win the game by matching your total score with the random number displayed, you lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game is reset, there will be a new random number and the value of the crystals will change also.
